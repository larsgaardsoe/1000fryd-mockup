The Mind Flowers are good news for all freaks and mutineers of the old world order. This psychedelic rock trio from Copenhagen excels at combining garage and psychedelia with an underlying love of melancholic blues.

A wise woman once said: “If I can’t dance, it’s not my revolution.” Fortunately there is plenty of dancing, lust and love in The Mind Flowers’ universe. You are hereby invited to shake everything you got!

Their contribution to the coming insurrection is delivered as a mix of psychedelic rock, beat and garage in the classic trio format. The sound is heavily inspired by the exploratory rock music of the 60s (such as 13th Floor Elevators, Hendrix and The West Coast Pop Art Experimental Band). However, The Mind Flowers mainly sound like themselves.
